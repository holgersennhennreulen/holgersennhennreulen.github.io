About:

This is my personal webpage. It is intended to help me organize and document some of my work as an applied statistician (nontraditional publications, like preprints, blog posts, supplementary websites, software, tutorials, workshops, outreach slides, simulation studies) as well as organize and document this same kind of work by other authors in the form of providing links to these resources.
The webpages name (If I’m doing an experiment to save the world, I better use my prior.) is a quote (listed here as no. 41 under http://www.stat.columbia.edu/~gelman/book/gelman_quotes.pdf ) by Statistician Prof. Andrew Gelman from Columbia University, and the short title of this webpage (I better use my prior.) is taken from this quote.
