About

This is my personal webpage, intended to help me organize some of my work as an applied statistician (preprints, teaching materials, code snippets, ...), but mostly I want to actually use it as a structured list with which I can organize work - the interesting stuff - of other authors in the form of providing links to these resources.
This webpage's name (If I’m doing an experiment to save the world, I better use my prior.) is a quote (listed here as no. 41 under http://www.stat.columbia.edu/~gelman/book/gelman_quotes.pdf ) by Statistician Prof. Andrew Gelman from Columbia University, and the short title of this webpage (I better use my prior.) is taken from this quote.
